Strawberry Lemonade

This recipe offers a delightful blend of fresh strawberries and zesty lemons, creating a sweet and tangy homemade Strawberry Lemonade. After blending and straining the strawberries, they're combined with lemon juice, sugar, and water, then chilled for optimal flavor. Garnish with lemon slices and mint for an elegant touch. Adjust sugar levels to taste, ensuring a refreshing beverage perfect for sipping on hot summer days.
